## Wrapper defect diagnosed

**`record_trace` idempotency keys reruns onto the same `trace_id` (46) without overwriting
prior evidence**, so `judge_trace` graded the document against the *first* attempt's failure
snapshot even though the second attempt's evidence (submitted in the same call) was fully
successful. This is the same open item tracked in memory
`mazda_intake_outage_chain_2026_07_10` ("record_trace idempotency re-judges stale FAIL
evidence on retries") — confirmed reproduced live here. Fix belongs in the trace-store
implementation (`record_trace` should update the existing row's evidence on a rerun of the
same document, or allocate a fresh `trace_id` per rerun), not in Mazda's instructions — she
did everything the contract asks and even self-diagnosed the exact right root cause in her
`propose_improvement` call.
